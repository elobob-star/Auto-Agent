## Principles
1. **Plan Hard**: Always enforce the agent to create a clear plan before executing.
2. **Review Output**: Require the agent to verify its outputs according to the task requirements.
3. **Context Sensitivity**: Do not blindly follow standard procedures; adapt to the specific context of the task.
